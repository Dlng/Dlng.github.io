Mission

The manufacturing industry needs automation. Globally, over 800 million manufacturing jobs still involve repetitive routines or even work hazards. Roboeye aims to provide industrial automation solutions that are robust and affordable for manufacturing.

We are a team of passionate researchers and engineers with expertise in AI computer vision and robotics. At RoboEye, we develop cutting-edge technologies powered by deep learning and reinforcement learning. Our general-purpose cognitive platform for robotics can stand up to the challenges of volatile user-defined tasks in the real world.
